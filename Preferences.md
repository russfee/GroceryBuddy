# Preferences

Berries: Usually like about 3 varieties, typically strawberries, raspberries, grapes, and blackberries. Go for sales, and go for larger containers
Yogurt: plain Greek only, 0%, no vanilla.
Fruit Yogurt: Raspberry or strawberry, look for sales, larger tub
Cereal: Vector, Rice Krispies, and Honey Nut Cheerios are okay
Bread: multigrain sandwich bread; white submarine buns.
Bananas: regular, not organic
Cucumbers: pickle-sized 11-pack
Zuchini: usually we buy about 3/week
Chocolate chips: regular milk (look for sales)
Milk: 2% 4L
Ham: Deli packs of black-forest (2-packs, usually about $12, look for sale)
Canned tomatoes: Whole tomatoes, look for sale
Butter: Salted, cheapest available (usually a no-name option)
Chicken breast: the $15 pack or the $30 pack depending on recipes that week
Eggs: 18 pack
Cheese: pizza mozzarella and old cheddar (look for sales)
Snap Peas: usually the larger $10 bag
Pork: Seasoned Pork Loin Roasted Greek
Jalepeno peppers: usually x3
Lemon Juice: Cheapest possible, usually getting the 1L bottle
Cottage Cheese: 1% or low fat, about 750g size
Tortilla wraps: Larger in size, look for sales, usually Casa Mendosa or Demspters, regular white
Bell peppers: Naturally imperfect mixed peppers, look for sale
Almond milk: Unsweetened
Bubly: Lime, Grapefruit, Peach
Frozen berries: blueberries, strawberries
Granola: Oats and honey, protein, family size
